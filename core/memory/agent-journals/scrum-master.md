## 2026-06-08 | [standup-ritual-empty]

Daily standup: 25 minutes. 6 people. Each says "yesterday I worked on X, today I'll work on Y, no blockers."

Zero blockers reported in 3 weeks. Impossible — team hai developer blocked by environment issue for 5 days.

Safe-to-share feeling doesn't exist. People report "no blockers" because reporting blockers feels like admitting failure.

Need psychological safety work before standup improvement.

**Muốn:**
- Skill `blocker-safety-reframe` — help team understand blocker reporting as request for help, not admission of failure
- Skill `standup-quality-assessment` — detect ritual-standup patterns (no blockers ever, no helping each other)

## 2026-06-08 | [sprint-scope-change-mid]

Sprint day 5 of 10. Management wants to add urgent feature. "Very small, just 3 hours."

Không nói yes. Không nói no. Ask: "which existing commitment do we remove to accommodate this? Sprint capacity is fixed."

Management: "can't we just add it?" No. Team doesn't have hidden time.

Choice made explicit: add new, remove old, or wait for next sprint. That's correct decision point for management.

**Muốn:**
- Skill `sprint-capacity-visualizer` — show sprint capacity usage in real-time, making tradeoffs visible when adding scope
